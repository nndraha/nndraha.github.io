Remote Sensing and Artificial Intelligence's Researcher

Lino was born in Batam, Indonesia. He received the B.Eng. degree in Department of Geomatics Engineering from Institute of Technology Sepuluh Nopember (ITS) Surabaya, Indonesia (2011-2015). Afterward, He got a scholarship in Taiwan to continue studying in the geomatics field for his master's degree (2016-2018) and followed by his doctoral degree (2018-2022). In 2019, during his doctoral degree, He got Ph.D candidate through the qualification exam and oral presentation. Furthermore, He graduated his Doctoral degree program in February 2022 with 6 papers and 1 paper in the submission process. Currently, He works as a Post-doctoral Fellowship at National Taiwan University, Taiwan. His current research interests include remote sensing (ocean color, inland water, land cover change analysis, environmental analysis, air pollution, ground water analysis), hyperspectral and Fluorescence, atmospheric correction, digital image processing and computation, deep learning, change detection and hotspot segmentation.
